In the print modal, set "Margins" to "None" and make sure to keep the "Background graphics" option enabled. --- # Details --- ## Options There are two options: * `mode` should be either `draft` or `presentation`. * `aspectRatio` defines the aspect ratio of your slides. ``` ``` Define your options *before* including Markdeep. --- ## Tips I recommend using only local resources (images, fonts, a local copy of mathjax) unless you're absolutely sure you'll have internet connectivity where you'll be holding your presentation. * To download fonts from Google fonts, use https://google-webfonts-helper.herokuapp.com/fonts. * A guide on running MathJax locally can be found at http://docs.mathjax.org/en/latest/installation.html. Note that you'll have to add `detectMath: false` to your Markdeep config then.
